What is Ukrainian Vyshyvanka?

Vyshyvanka is a kind of traditional clothing in Ukraine. It is a part of the Ukrainian national costume, and also a piece of clothing often recreated in the modern fashionable version. Since Ukraine restored its independence in 1991, vyshyvanka became super popular again. If you wonder how to wear a vyshyvanka, the answer is quite simple.
